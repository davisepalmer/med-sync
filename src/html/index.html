Your dashboard for sharing hospital resources

Across the globe, lower income hospitals are at a severe disadvantage due to lack of funding leading to a lack of medicine, supplies, and skilled staff. Medi-Connect aims to bridge this gap by providing a platform for hospitals to share resources including, but not limited to, hospital beds, medicine, a skilled second opinion, and general supplies.
